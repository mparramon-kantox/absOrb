Instructions

In absOrb, the goal is to score as many points as possible within the given amount of moves or time.

You score points by matching two adjacent orbs to make a horizontal or vertical match of 3 or more of the same colour orb.

The orbs will then cascade down from the top with new random orbs being created to fill the gaps. The orbs will continue to cascade until there are no matches left.

The more match combos you make in one turn, the higher the score multiplier will be for that turn, earning you more points.
