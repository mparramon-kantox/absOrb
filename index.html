Instructions

In absOrb, the goal is to score as many points as possible within the given amount of moves or time.

You score points by switching two adjacent orbs to make a horizontal or vertical match of 3 or more of the same colour orbs.

The orbs will then cascade down from the top with new random orbs being created to fill the gaps. The orbs will continue to cascade until there are no matches left.

The more match combos you make in one turn, the higher the score multiplier will be for that turn, earning you more points. You also gain extra multipliers for matching a row of 4 or 5.

If you manage to match a row of 5, a power Orb will appear. When matched with a colour, this orb will destroy all orbs on the board with the same colour.
